Final Design Direction: Photos Edge-to-Edge

Breakthrough Solution: Removed all padding/margin from album photos so they cover the entire card edge-to-edge. No need for a separate card background color - the photos ARE the card. Completely eliminates the background color confusion issue.

Background Gradient: Using the original dark blue gradient from refined.html: #0a1520 ‚Üí #12242e ‚Üí #0f1d28. Provides subtle color interest while staying dark and sophisticated.

Album Cards: Photos extend completely to edges (padding: 0). Only 8px gap between the 4 photos creates subtle grid. Text overlay positioned over the bottom photos with dark gradient. Much cleaner and simpler - no secondary background needed.

Visual Separation: Cards separated by 32px gap in the grid. The photos themselves provide all the visual mass. On hover, card lifts with shadow which creates clear depth and separation from the page background.

Text Overlay: Dark gradient (rgba(10, 21, 32, 0.98)) ensures white text readability. On hover, transitions to brand blue gradient (rgba(24, 89, 134, 0.95)) for interactive feedback. Photos darken slightly (brightness 0.85) to make text even more prominent.

Typography: Georgia serif for album names (elegant, luxury feel). Courier New monospace for dates and metadata (technical precision). Light weights (300) work beautifully on dark backgrounds.

Why This Works: Simplest solution - let the photos do the work. No confusing secondary background colors. Photos edge-to-edge creates bold, confident cards that float against the dark blue page gradient. The 8px gaps between photos add just enough structure without feeling heavy.

Hover Interaction: Card lifts, photos darken, text overlay becomes blue wash, shadow appears. Provides clear interactive feedback with elegant transitions. Album count changes from cyan to white for extra polish.

Responsive Behavior: Same approach works on mobile - photos scale naturally, text remains readable with gradient overlay. 32px gap between cards provides comfortable touch targets.
