Design Direction v3: Dark Teal Background

Background Gradient (Darkened Teal): Based on analogous color #114a41 but significantly darkened to keep background subtle. Creates: #0a1613 ‚Üí #0d2420 ‚Üí #0b1b18. Very dark teal that stays close to black while adding subtle color interest. Much more restrained than v2.

Album Cards (Lighter Teal): Cards use lighter version of same teal family: #143832 ‚Üí #1a4840. Still teal-based (not grey) to harmonize with background, but noticeably lighter for clear differentiation. Cards are the same hue as background, just brighter.

Color Harmony: Background is very dark teal (~10-15% lightness), cards are medium-dark teal (~20-25% lightness). Same hue family creates cohesive color system. Brand blue (#185986) used for accents and hover states provides complementary color contrast.

Text Over Photos: Album name and metadata positioned over 2√ó2 photo grid. Gradient overlay uses darkest teal (rgba(10, 22, 19, 0.98)) for readability. On hover, transitions to brand blue tint (rgba(24, 89, 134, 0.95)).

Why This Works: Dark teal background feels sophisticated and modern without being as harsh as pure black. Lighter teal cards clearly stand out while maintaining color harmony. Brand blue accents pop beautifully against teal foundation. Overall palette feels cohesive and intentional.

Typography: Georgia serif for album names (elegant), Courier New monospace for dates (technical). Light font weights (300) work well on dark backgrounds. Album count in cyan (#6ab9de) transitions to white on hover.

Hover Interactions: Card lifts with shadow, photos darken slightly, text overlay transitions from dark teal to brand blue wash. Provides clear interactive feedback while maintaining sophistication.
